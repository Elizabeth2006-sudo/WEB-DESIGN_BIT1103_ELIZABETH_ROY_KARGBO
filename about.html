About the Academy

Mission, history & achievements

General Information

The Football Academy of Sierra Leone is the premier institution for developing young football talent nationwide. We provide comprehensive training programs for aspiring athletes.

Our programs focus on technical skills, tactical understanding, physical conditioning and mental preparation. Learn more about our methodology.

Agency Partners

We collaborate with national and international organisations to provide the best opportunities for our players. These partnerships help us access advanced training techniques and competitive platforms.

View our partner directory to see all organisations supporting our mission.

Agency Personnel

Our team consists of experienced coaches, sports scientists and administrative professionals dedicated to football development. Each staff member brings unique expertise to our academy.

Meet our coaching staff and learn about their qualifications.

Agency History

Founded in 2010, the Football Academy of Sierra Leone has grown from a small training centre to a nationally recognised institution. Our journey reflects the growing passion for football in Sierra Leone.

Explore our timeline to see key milestones in our development.

Agency Achievements

Our academy has produced numerous players who have gone on to successful professional careers both domestically and internationally. We take pride in their accomplishments.
